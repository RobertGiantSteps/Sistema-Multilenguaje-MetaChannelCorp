Legal-Technological Services of META Channel Corporation

META Channel Corporation is a global legal-technological ecosystem that transforms regulatory complexity into international scalability. With a presence in 30 countries and more than 150 experts, we offer comprehensive solutions in technology law, financial regulation, and cross-border corporate strategy.

Our approach combines legal rigor with technological implementation, enabling our clients to operate safely across multiple jurisdictions. From asset tokenization to AI Act and MiCA regulatory compliance, every service is designed to deliver measurable results.

Our Strategic Capabilities

  • Global Regulatory Strategy — Regulatory compliance in the European Union, the United States, and Latin America
  • Technological Implementation — Development of LegalTech solutions and automation of legal processes
  • Asset Tokenization — Legal and technical structuring for token issuance under MiCA and local regulations
  • Litigation Finance — Strategic litigation funding at the international level
  • Global Law and Investment — Cross-border M&A advisory and international corporate structuring

International Offices
